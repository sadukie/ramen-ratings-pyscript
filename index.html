Playing with PyScript and the Ramen Ratings data from Kaggle

Brands Most Tested

import pandas as pd from pyodide.http import open_url url_content = open_url("https://raw.githubusercontent.com/sadukie/ramen-ratings-pyscript/main/ramen-ratings.csv") df = pd.read_csv(url_content) df.Brand.value_counts().nlargest(10).to_frame().to_html()
